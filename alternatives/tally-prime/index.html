FAQ

TallyPrime alternative questions

Is Fastbooks replacing TallyPrime?

Not necessarily. The sharper positioning is that Fastbooks reduces daily accounting entry preparation. Accountants can still review the final accounts process.

Why would a business need Fastbooks if it already uses Tally?

Because the daily data-entry layer is still painful: bills, expenses, bank transactions, narration and party matching. Fastbooks targets that capture-and-prepare workflow.

Can accountants use Fastbooks?

Yes. The review inbox is designed for accountants who need control before entries become final.
