# Fastbooks SEO Roadmap

This sitemap expansion is inspired by high-performing Indian accounting software pages, but the content is original and positioned around Fastbooks' wedge: accounting entry preparation from daily inputs.

## Competitor patterns we are using

### From listicle pages
- "Best accounting software in India"
- "Most used accounting software in India"
- Comparison tables
- How to choose sections
- FAQs
- Tool/provider lists

### From commercial product pages
- GST billing
- Inventory accounting
- e-Invoicing
- e-Way bill
- Mobile accounting app
- PC/Mac/ERP/ledger terms

### From industry pages
- Retail
- Wholesale
- Manufacturing
- Service businesses
- Restaurants
- Pharmacy
- FMCG
- Textile
- Jewellery

## Fastbooks positioning rule

Avoid claiming we already replace every full accounting system.

Use this safe positioning:

> Fastbooks helps Indian businesses prepare review-ready accounting entries from bills, receipts, bank transactions and voice notes.
